Have a question? Interested in working with us? Want to schedule a conversation? Here's how to get in touch.

By email

Our primary point of contact is our email address, info@projectscifi.org. If you have questions or you are thinking about getting involved, let us know in an email and we'll let you know where to go from there.

By phone or video call

We would love to have a live conversation with you! If you are interested in sponsoring or receiving lab coats, this is the fastest and easiest way to get things rolling. Please email us at info@projectscifi.org and let us know when you are available to talk with us.

Through social media

As a quick and easy alternative to email, you can also contact us on Facebook or Twitter to ask questions or schedule a conversation.

By physical mail

To get a physical address, please contact us at info@projectscifi.org.
