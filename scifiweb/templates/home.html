Bringing lab gear to schools in need

Project SCIFI works together with schools and sponsors to promote science education at American high schools and expose students to real careers in science.

Mission

The Scientific Coat Initiative for Future Innovators seeks to promote the pursuit of students’ scientific passions in higher education, to provide teachers the resources necessary to create opportunities for hands-on scientific education, to create a visible community supporting young scientists’ endeavors, and to inspire future influential mentor/mentee relationships.

We achieve these goals by working with corporate and academic sponsors who endorse the purchase of customized laboratory coats embroidered with the sponsor’s logo which we then distribute to schools in need along with other resources necessary for the educators to successfully teach hands-on science.

We are a non-profit organization comprised of scientists shaped by our own experiences pursuing a scientific education who strive to inspire the courage future scientists and innovators will need to pursue their passions.
